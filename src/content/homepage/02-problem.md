heading: "Here's the thing about getting a website"
---

Most small businesses know they need a website, but the process feels overwhelming. Too many options, too many cookie-cutter solutions that slap a template on every business like they're all the same. What's missing is strategy: understanding what your site needs to *achieve*. More customers? More bookings? Better leads? If your website isn't driving those results, it's just expensive decoration.
